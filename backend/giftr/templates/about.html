About Us
Tired of receiving the same old gift sets every Birthday? Well look no further...
We are university students who have gifted you with your very own gift-finder to help you find the perfect gift for both your loved ones and yourself!
We know just how hard it is to find the perfect, unique gift and noticed there was a lack of help out there.
And thus, Giftr was born.
We help you find the best gifts by creating wishlists and use artificial intelligence, be it for your mum or your better half.
We are certain these gifts will bring them joy as our very smart AI helps to keep it a surprise.
It will also make it easier for your loved ones to see what to get you so we can avoid the dreaded lynx sets or boring chocolate boxes.
Use the provided filters to ease your search, be it fitting into a budget or knowing what category you want to find the gift in
